Mughlai Chicken (Taylor's Version)

Mughlai Chicken


“A Taylor Swift Favorite”- Elle Magazine

Description

“I’ve always cooked a lot, but I found three recipes I know I’ll be making at dinner parties for life.” writes Dr. Taylor Alison Swift in her interview with Elle Magazine. One of those recipes is Nigella Lawson’s Mughlai Chicken. Bringing together a troop of well-balanced spices such as cumin, ginger, coriander, and chili -- we’re guessing this is Dr. Swift’s go-to dish during the first fall of snow on a glistening December night.

Nigella Lawson strongly recommends using the moister brown meat, from the thigh, for this dish. For full depth and resonance of flavour, she states to make it a day or two in advance and stored in the fridge. The payoff is totally worth it.
